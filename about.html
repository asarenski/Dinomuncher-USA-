Introduction

65 Million Years Ago....
T-rex dinomuncher is at the height of his evolution during the late Cretaceous. His bite strength is the strongest of any land-dwelling animal in history, ten times stronger than a Gator's. It's time for you to create him in all his dinomunching glory! But something dangerous and unknown lies in the heavens....

This website has been created to provide a guide. The scope includes Python and setting-up its environment in Ubuntu. If you are interested in learning how to use the Tkinter module in Python. I encourage you to follow the tutorial shown in Dinomuncher.

Dinomuncher shows some of the capabilities of Tkinter, which can be used to create a simple GUI for a game or a program. If you are a true beginner I encourage you to thoroughly review the Requirements. Requirements also contains a basic tutorial on setting up a Python environment in Ubuntu.
